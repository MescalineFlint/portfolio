Alfa Space Invasion is one of the best games for space lovers. Fans of science fiction films about alien invasions will be able to witness the event and take an active part in the battle. Alpha Sector is in danger. Aliens are about to arrive. You will be the young pilot at the helm of the spacecraft. You’re going to have to fight alien enemies because they’re going to kill you and take over the galaxy. You’re in danger. In addition, you will encounter asteroids. You never know what’s more dangerous...
